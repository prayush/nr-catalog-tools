# NR Catalog Statistics

The following table breaks down the simulations in the SXS, RIT, and GT (MAYA) catalogs by their spin configuration and initial eccentricity.

**Definitions:**
- **Non-spinning**: Magnitude of both dimensionless spins is $< 10^{-4}$.
- **Aligned-spinning**: Not non-spinning, and all in-plane spin components ($x$ and $y$) are $< 10^{-4}$.
- **Precessing-spinning**: All other spin configurations.
- **Non-eccentric**: Initial eccentricity $< 0.005$.
- **Eccentric**: Initial eccentricity $\ge 0.005$.
